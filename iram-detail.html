L'IRAM

Project Overview

During my UX design training, I developed skills in adaptive design. Unlike responsive design, the adaptive approach focuses on the core of the user experience, taking into account all specific needs on mobile. This allows for truly tailored interfaces, enhancing accessibility and user satisfaction. I led a complete redesign of the IRAM website, also creating personas to craft a new optimal user experience despite the site's complexity, with its numerous pages and varied links. This project allowed me to strengthen my ability to manage ambitious projects while remaining focused on user expectations.
