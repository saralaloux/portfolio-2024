Proximus

Project Overview

Here is the redesign of the Proximus.be website, carried out as part of my UX design training. The main challenge of this project was to identify areas for improvement on an already well-developed site, while preserving its visual identity and adhering to a particularly comprehensive style guide. It involved rethinking certain aspects of the user experience to optimize navigation and provide a more intuitive interface, while remaining true to the company's high standards. The goal of this exercise is to demonstrate my ability to undertake an ambitious project while respecting best practices, combining creativity with technical precision.
